Civilian Spaceflight: Making Space Travel More Accessible

we are now launching civilian flybys and small missions to the Moon at the price of around $100,000 for a suborbital trip, and $10 million for a full LEO trip. We are making space travel more accessible than ever before. With our affordable prices and innovative technology, We are opening up the cosmos to a whole new generation of explorers.

Suborbital Trips

A suborbital trip is a flight that takes you to the edge of space and back. You will experience weightlessness and see the curvature of the Earth. A suborbital trip with Nexus lasts for about 10 minutes.

LEO Trips

An LEO trip is a flight that takes you to an altitude of about 100 miles above Earth. You will orbit the Earth for about 90 minutes.
